About

  • ABOUT
    DokeV

    What is DokeV? DokeV is an upcoming creature-collecting open world action-adventure from Pearl Abyss filled with intriguing stories and set in a unique, vibrant world.

    Meet Dokebi, whimsical creatures that live side-by-side with humans and encourage people's dreams from which they gain strength.

    The gorgeous and colorful world of DokeV is brimming with activities, stories, and experiences, so get ready to befriend tons of Dokebi, embark on an adventure, and rock the world! Who knows, they may be dancing around you, even now..!

    Wondering how to pronounce DokeV? Read it as "doh-keh-vee". It's the world where the Dokebi live in! Hear it all first on DokeV's social media or by subscribing to the official newsletter on the site.

  • About
    Pearl Abyss

    Founded in 2010, Pearl Abyss is a global game developer known for the Game franchise. We are currently servicing Game across 150 regions on PC, console, and mobile, and developing Crimson Desert, DokeV and PLAN 8 with Pearl Abyss’ proprietary next-generation engine.

    Our goal is to create an unforgettable experience with extraordinary gameplay. Aspiring to create a realistic and immersive game for players, we work with the highest standards of systems, game graphics, and sound.
