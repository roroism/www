ABOUT US

Climbing and skiing are all about Black Diamond. We've had a lot of experience together on rock, ice and snowy mountains, and this experience allows us to build the best equipment in existence for climbers and skiers all over the world.Black Diamond, which started with a hammer and anvil in the backyard of Yvonne Chenard's house, and has grown into a global company with offices on three continents, is not just a company that makes rock climbing equipment. Black Diamond is a company that supports and upholds the spirit of the sport we engage in, its values and purpose throughout the past, present and future.

Since 1957, our innovative machine designs have set new milestones in countless fields. This is not only the result of the dedication, passion and hard work of our development team of amazing people, but also because we are climbers and skiers ourselves. Black Diamond is a company of users. We are them. We are dreamers of grand dreams of how far our products can evolve, and we are also the most ruthless critics of what exists today. Therefore, there is no end to the process of developing new products and new technologies at Black Diamond.

Now Black Diamond is stronger and more immersed than ever. The future of Black Diamond, a company for climbers and skiers all over the world, is very promising, fueled by the boundless energy and passion of the many people working in Black Diamond's US headquarters, Black Diamond Europe and Black Diamond Asia.
